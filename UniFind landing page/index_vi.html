Giới thiệu

UniFind is a software project that aim to build an online tool for students, universities, and companies which helps them meet their needs and requirements.

Student can use Unifind's Test to explore their personalities, giving result of their key strengths as well as their soft skills and some advices with specialization and career path in future.

For universities & companies, Unifind provides an online recruitment gateway where they can advertise themselves as well as giving recruitment requirements to possibly find the best one on needs.
